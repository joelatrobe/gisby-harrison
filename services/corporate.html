Practice area 01

Corporate

Our corporate team advises on all aspects of creating, running, trading as, and disposing of a business.

We act mainly for developing entrepreneurs and family-managed unlisted companies, bringing commercial insight to every stage of a business's life.

Mergers, acquisitions, buy-outs and disposals

We specialise in the buying and selling of trading companies, with extensive experience representing both purchasers and sellers in these intricate transactions.

Company law

Our work in this area covers a wide range of matters:

  • Incorporation
  • Business structuring and reorganisation
  • Reconstructions
  • Financing and securities
  • Joint venture and partnership agreements
  • Share buy-backs and capital reductions
  • Share option schemes

Directors' duties and responsibilities

We provide guidance on regulatory compliance and on strategies to mitigate director liabilities.

Commercial law

Our services include drafting business terms, supply contracts, distribution and franchise agreements, e-commerce arrangements, and a range of other trading agreements.

We work closely with our employment and commercial property specialists internally, and with external advisers in pensions, corporate finance and taxation, so you receive joined-up advice whenever your matter needs it.
