Employment Law

Few areas of law have been subject to more new UK and European legislation than that regulating the rights of employees and the responsibilities of employers.

We advise both employers, from large corporations to sole traders, and employees. Seeing both sides gives us the perspective to offer genuinely comprehensive support.

For employers

  • Directors' service agreements
  • Employee contracts and staff handbooks
  • Redundancy procedures
  • Disciplinary procedures
  • Termination of contracts and severance agreements
  • Business transfers
  • Drafting and enforcing restrictive covenants
  • Discrimination claims
  • Maternity and parental leave
  • Representation at tribunals and before the courts

For employees

  • Negotiation and advice on contracts
  • Advice on the enforceability of restrictive covenants
  • Assistance with disciplinary and grievance procedures
  • Advice on all employee claims, including breach of contract, unfair dismissal, redundancy and discrimination
  • Severance and settlement agreements
